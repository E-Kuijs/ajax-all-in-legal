Delete Account – Ajax All-In

Users of the Ajax All-In app can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to:

All.Inn.Media.contact@gmail.com

Include the email address used in the app and your username (if available).

What will be deleted

  • User account
  • Profile information
  • Associated personal data stored in the app database

Requests are normally processed within 30 days.
