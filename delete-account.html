Delete Account – Ajax All-In Last updated: 2026 Users of the Ajax All-In app can request deletion of their account and associated personal data. How to request account deletion To request deletion of your account, send an email to: All.Inn.Media.contact@gmail.com Please include: The email address used in the app Your username (if available) What will be deleted When your request is processed, the following data may be removed: Your user account Profile information Chat messages and related user-generated content where technically possible Associated personal data stored in the app database Retention Some data may be retained for a limited period if required for: security abuse prevention legal obligations Account deletion requests are normally processed within 30 days. Contact If you have questions about your data or privacy, contact: All-Inn Media Email: All.Inn.Media.contact@gmail.com
